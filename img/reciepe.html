Recipe

This food is the Italian pork and beef meat ball with polenta. This dish is good for the winter season. It is simply made by mixing the pork and beef mince together with finely chopped herbs like mint, parsley, rosemary, sage etc. and finely grated parmesan cheese with slight seasoning of salt and pepper. Then we make the balls from the mixture of mince and cook it with napolitana sauce (cooked tomato sauce with online and basil) for an hour. Then for polenta we boil the mixture half and half of milk and water. Then we add the polenta small amount at a time till the mixture thickens. Then we put the polenta on the base of the plate with meat balls on top of it and we ad grated cheese and parley as a garnish.

This dish is the rigatoni carbonara pasta. This is one of the delicious pastas of the Italian cuisine. To make this pasta. First put the rigatoni pasta in the hot boiling water. Then the pasta will be cooked by 9 minutes then immediately take the pasta out of the boiling water and put some oil in it so that the pasta does not stick with each other. Then chop the Bacon in small pieces. Fry the bacon till it become crispy put little bit of water to the fried bacon so that it does not dries out. Take some eggs and separate yolk and egg white and mix egg yolk with the equal amount of the pure cream. Then add the cream yolk mix on the pan with pasta and toss it till it becomes creamy. Add some cheese and seasoning to get the taste. And plate it as on the photo with the garnish of the grated parmesan and parsley.
